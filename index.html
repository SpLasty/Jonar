Why Am I a fit for this job

Cultural Fit

I resonate a lot with the article, posted on Jonar's blog, titled "Leading with Imposter Syndrome". I have struggled with imposter syndrome, being fearful of the unknown future. I was afraid to step out of comfort zone. But there was a quote from the movie Inception which stuck with me and was an eye opener. "Would you like to take a leap of faith or become an old man filled with regret, waiting to die alone". I wouldn't say there was an instant effect, but it hit me and I knew I had to take risks in order to succeed. Since, then I have boarded the plane and flew all the way from Bangladesh to Canada(which was essentially my first flight ever) and I have learned to adjust here in a completely new environment. In terms of cultural fit, I am adaptable to new settings which help me fit easily into a new ecosystem without disrupting it. I am no longer afraid to ask the questions and make mistakes in order to learn more and succeed eventually.

Job Fit

I am very passionte when it comes to software and web development. I like to build projects on my own during my free time to facilitate my learning. I am still on the learning process and I am actively learning new technology stacks and improving on my skills. While I lack experience, I have the desire and hunger to learn and I think that sets me up as a right candidate. I am also willing to relocate if required for the job.
